Graduating from Lisbon’s Lusíada University in 1987, Luís Reigadas started practicing law as a trainee. He also hold a position as a legal advisor for his graduating university in September 1988, a position held until April 1989, when he had the opportunity to serve as a Public Prosecutor Agent in Miranda do Douro.

In January 1990, he moved to Macau to work as a legal advisor to the President of the Ilhas Urban Council. He was also a legal advisor in the Public Administration Social Services from June 1990 until July 1991, a legal advisor to the Secretary of Macau Government Health and Social Affairs Department from September 1991 until June 1992, and to the Macau Consumers Council from September 1998 until 2005, and is since June 1990 the legal advisor to Macau Government Social Security Fund. In addition to his work as legal advisor, Luís Reigadas has lectured in the Macau Polytechnic Institute

In 1991, along with the late Luís Serpa Soares he founded our office and since then have been practicing law in Macau as a lawyer and as a private notary, being a member of the Macau Lawyers Association and of the Portuguese Bar Association.
